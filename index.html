Welcome

This site is a brief summary of me and what I like to do. I recently moved to Chicago on a one way trip to attend Dev Bootcamp. Which is a 9 week crash course on web development. I love spending time learning to code, but free time is always so limited and precious. I spent the majority on my technical life learning networking, it seemed like an easier field to break into with my background. But my passion for raw code kept sidetracking me, even leading me to an attempt to join the Navy. Until a friend suggested Dev Bootcamp, saying it will make me hirable within a few months. I was saving money for a new truck at the time, and I made the decision to make a change in my life for the better. I sold my truck, left my job as a yacht technician, and a few months later, here I am. I wanted to live by the old adage "If you do something you love, you will never work a day in your life".

Although, I do miss Florida's sunny weather sometimes.

Why Am I Here

I love it all! Hardware, software, networks, servers, applications, switches, routers, OSs, APIs, OOP. Its a good feeling that I am able to traverse the whole OSI model. I like talking about chipsets, I love how modular applications are with the use of APIs, it fascinates me how dynamically windowing will let a server spit out unack packets. The Internet is truly something special, it can reach out and change peoples lives. Anything you want to learn is at the touch of your fingertips, and I am wholeheartedly glad to be apart of it.
